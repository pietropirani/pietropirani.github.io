Jéssica Pirani

Artistic Photography

I consider myself as a Traveler, Lifestyle and Portrait photographer currently based in Cardiff, Wales. In the past few years, while living in Berlin, I've traveled to some interesting countries around the globe and discovered Photography as a passion, as something I wanted to do for the rest of my life. I really like to do artistic photos, play with colors and shades and run away from the basic stuff.

I'm available for domestic and international opportunities, always open for partnerships and looking forward to try new things, visit new places and discover new shapes of art. Below you can see a few examples of my work and if you want to see more, follow me on Instagram and you will be up to date with the current work that I've been doing.
